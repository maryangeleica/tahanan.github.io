our menu popular menu

Americano Coffee

has a bold flavor and can be enjoyed black or with a splash of cream or milk.

Vanilla Iced Coffee

It is a combination of cold-brewed coffee with ice, milk, and a hint of vanilla. It is light, creamy and full of flavor.

Classic Caramel Frappe

This decadent frozen beverage, made with espresso and creamy caramel syrup, is a favorite of many coffee lovers. The frappe is a creamy, sweet, and rich drink that has a flavor that is simply irresistible.

Greek Frappe

Is a great way to start off your day or to provide a refreshing pick-me-up in the afternoon. It is a great drink to share with friends and family or to simply enjoy alone.

Passionfruit Lemonades

Passionfruit is a tropical fruit that has a sweet, floral taste while lemonades are a tart, refreshing beverage. Combining these two flavors creates a unique flavor, and it's the perfect summer beverage.

Ube Milktea

It is made by combining brewed tea with ube extract, a purple yam that is known for its sweet taste and vibrant color. The drink is often served with a scoop of tapioca pearls and a sweetened creamer for added texture and flavor.

Brownies

Chocolate brownies are the most popular, featuring a rich, fudgy texture and intense chocolate flavor.

Oreo Cookies & Cream Cupcake

The combination of chocolate, cream cheese, and Oreo cookies is simply irresistibleand can be enjoyed any time.

about tahanan

What Makes Tahanan Unique?

not just an ordinary coffee shop and it is more than you expected! Tahanan offers variety of menus and there are 5 rooms for you to choose from:

Kinig

If you want a place to just let the music connect with your emotions while sitting and sipping a cup of coffee, then this is perfect for you!

Kanta

is where you can sing your heart out! Don’t be afraid to belt out a tune and let your voice be heard!

Basa

is where you can read books. If you are a bookworm then no doubt, this place truly suits you!

Pinta

allows you to paint in a canvas and let the strokes flow naturally. Allowing oneself to be creative and explore with different colors and shapes can be a great way to express one’s inner artist and explore the possibilities of art.

Nood

is where you can sit and watch movies. Movies can provide a break from study or work, allowing one to refocus and clear their mind.
